Without changing the design, briefly compare the Login and Signup wireframes. What's the visual relationship between them, and what 1 small thing would you change in Signup to make it feel more cohesive with Login?

Relationship: Both use the same low-fi paper aesthetic — Caveat headings, Kalam body, mono labels, black ink, single orange accent, and the same hand-drawn field/button primitives. The variation numbering and names map across (Centered, Split, Editorial appear in both; Floating Card is shared; Login has a Stepped variant, Signup has no equivalent).

Divergences:

  • Login shows 5 options, Signup shows 4.

One small change for cohesion: add a 5th Signup variation that mirrors Login's "Stepped" (name → email → password, one field per step). That would make the two sets a true 1:1 pair and reinforce that either flow can be chosen as a system rather than per-screen.
